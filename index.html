Welcome to Enlight!

  • Double-click the canvas to create a shape.
  • Double-click on a shape to delete it.
  • Click on a shape to select it.
  • Move a selected shape by dragging it.
  • Resize a selected shape by dragging its corners.
  • Click on the canvas to deselect any shapes.
  • Open this panel at any time by clicking '?'.
  • Click anywhere to close this panel.

Now create some shapes,
wander around & enjoy the view!
